## Common Questions

**Q: How long are typical engagements?**
A: Our engagements range from short-term projects (1-3 months) to ongoing content partnerships. We customize based on your needs and content goals.

**Q: Can you work with my existing marketing team?**
A: Absolutely! We collaborate closely with your team and help them master AI tools and best practices through training and mentoring.

**Q: What content types do you create?**
A: We create blog posts, landing pages, email campaigns, social media content, product descriptions, and more—all optimized for SEO and conversions.

**Q: How do you approach SEO?**
A: We combine AI-powered content creation with proven SEO best practices, including keyword research, on-page optimization, and performance tracking.

A: We work with ChatGPT, GPT-4, specialized AI content platforms, and a suite of SEO and marketing tools. We also help you select and implement the right tools for your business.
